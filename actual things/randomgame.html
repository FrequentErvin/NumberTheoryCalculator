Rules

Pressing "Solved" increases your score by 100, pressing "Skip" decreases your score by 75 and an unfortunate fakesolve decreases your score by 50.

Pressing the "END" button will automaticaly end the game and your score will be memorised at that point (bragging rights).

How it works

The problem difficulty is determined by your current score. Obviously, this isn't graded so try to be truthful for your own sake.

The problems are NOT original and all rights go to the respected authors (links or source to the previous shown problem is always near the bottom)
